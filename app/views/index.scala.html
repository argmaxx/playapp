Our Services

Software systems for advanced analytics

The definition of what's considered big data depends on the current analytic capabilities of your organization. If you are considering data and analytics beyond your existing capabilities, you have a big data problem. We can help you assess your current capabilities considering your immediate and future goals. We can then recommend, and optionally implement, a modern software system that satisfies your constraints for cost and functionality.

Analytic models for your decision tasks

While the software systems we deliver can be used by your in-house business analysts and data scientists directly, we can also work alongside them to develop models — especially prescriptive analytics — for your specific business needs. Traditional descriptive analytics help you understand what happened and what's happening whereas predictive analytics provide insight into what's likely to happen. Prescriptive analytics take you a step further and evaluate the expected effects of your decision options under the predicted scenarios.

Smart agents for the Internet of Things

Our solutions can serve as tools for human decision-makers or be incorporated into fully automated agent-based systems. We have a growing focus on the Internet of Things (IoT) within the context of distributed web-based software services and the people who interact with those things and services. If you would like to add adaptive and learning capabilities to your existing IoT products, or are considering new products or processes within your organization that exploit IoT opportunities, we can help you design and build such capabilities.

Our Platform

Best of breed technology

Choosing software components for your big data solution is difficult. We have evaluated scores of options and developed a modular platform that combines best of breed components with our own proprietary AI components to create an intelligent event-driven platform for data management, analytics processing and agent simulation.

Custom implementations

The ideal big data and prescriptive analytics architecture for your business depends on what you consider to be real-time optimization. We work with the requirements and constraints implied by that definition to customize and extend our platform as needed to best handle the volume, velocity and variety of the data to be analyzed.
